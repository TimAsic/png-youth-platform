Proposal Success Probability Model

Purpose

Estimate the likelihood that a proposal can be successfully implemented.

The goal is to move beyond popularity and measure practical viability.

---

Core Factors

Success Probability depends on:

- Support Level
- Geographic Reach
- Feasibility
- Estimated Cost
- Implementation Complexity

---

Support Score (SS)

SS = SupportCount / ActiveUsers

Measures public backing.

---

Geographic Reach Score (GRS)

GRS = SupportingProvinces / TotalProvinces

Measures national spread.

---

Feasibility Score (FS)

Range: 0–100

Factors:

- Technical feasibility
- Legal feasibility
- Resource availability

---

Cost Efficiency Score (CES)

Range: 0–100

Higher score = lower implementation cost relative to impact.

---

Complexity Score (CS)

Range: 0–100

Higher score = more difficult implementation.

---

Proposal Success Probability (PSP)

PSP =

(SS × 0.25)

+ (GRS × 0.20)

+ (FS × 0.30)

+ (CES × 0.15)

− (CS × 0.10)

---

Interpretation

PSP > 80

High Probability of Success

PSP 60–80

Moderate Probability

PSP 40–60

Low Probability

PSP < 40

Very Low Probability
