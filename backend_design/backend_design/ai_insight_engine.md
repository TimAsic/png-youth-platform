# AI Insight Engine

## Purpose

This system converts raw data (reports, proposals, statistics) into human-understandable insights.

It allows users without technical knowledge to understand national data.

---

## Core Principle

Raw data alone is not useful.

Insight must include:

- Simple explanation
- Meaning
- Impact
- Suggested actions

---

## Input Types

The AI Insight Engine processes:

- Institution reports
- Proposal data
- Provincial statistics
- Group discussions
- NDI outputs

---

## Output Structure

Each insight must generate:

### 1. Headline
Simple one-line summary

### 2. Simple Explanation
Easy language description

### 3. Why It Matters
Real-life impact

### 4. Community Signals
What people/groups are saying

### 5. Suggested Actions
Possible responses or solutions

---

## AI Insight Model

Insight =

Raw Data
→ Interpretation Layer
→ Context Layer
→ Action Layer

---

## Example Output

### Headline
Malaria cases increasing in Morobe Province

### Simple Explanation
More people are getting malaria compared to last year.

### Why It Matters
This can affect health, school attendance, and workforce productivity.

### Suggested Actions
- distribute mosquito nets
- improve rural health access
- increase awareness campaigns

---

## Role in System

This engine connects:

- Data (Institutions)
- Analysis (NDI system)
- Human understanding (citizens)
- Decision-making (government & NGOs)
